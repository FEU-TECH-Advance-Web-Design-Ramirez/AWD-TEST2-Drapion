Mission & Vision

Soul Speak provides community-curated mental healthcare services and solutions to workplaces, schools, and non-profits in order to improve the well-being of their communities.

We aspire to create mental healthcare support, programs, and technologies that create genuine and lasting impact in the mental health space. Our purpose is to stand as a well-being ally for the communities we work with, deliver quality support, and develop programs hand-in-hand with both our individual and organization clients.
