What is guided meditation?

Guided meditation is a form of meditation led by a teacher through audio, video, or in person. It helps beginners understand the mind, learn meditation techniques, and apply them in daily life. The process involves three steps: approach (understanding the mind), practice (learning techniques), and integration (applying meditation to everyday life).
